Distributions

__mctad.js__ can create many common discrete and continuous probability distributions from parameters you supply. The resulting distribution objects contain common statistics on the distribution (when defined); convenience methods for accessing probability distribution, probability mass, and cumulative probability functions; domain & range helpers to simplify integration with [D3](http://d3js.org/), and random variable generators. * Discrete Probability Distributions * [Discrete Uniform](/discrete/uniform/) * [Binomial](discrete/binomial/) * [Bernoulli](discrete/bernoulli/) * [Geometric](discrete/geometric/) * [Hypergeometric](discrete/hypergeometric/) * [Pascal](discrete/pascal/) * [Poisson](discrete/poisson/) * Continuous Probability Distributions * [Uniform](/continuous/uniform/) * [Triangular](/continuous/triangular/) * [Exponential](/continuous/exponential/) * [Normal](/continuous/normal/) * [Lognormal](/continuous/lognormal/) * [Student's t](/continuous/students_t/)
